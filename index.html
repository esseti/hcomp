Purpose

We bring different communities working in human computation together by means of a slack group. With this Slack group we target a world-wide audience of researchers, industry players, practitioners and crowd workers.

The members of the community:
  • announce calls for collaborations, proposals, organization of HC-related venues
  • promote experiments and attract participants
  • discuss and followup on works presented at conferences/meetings/workshops
  • organise meetings of local representatives
  • meet others working in specific narrow field.
  • promote relevant jobs at companies and academic institutions
